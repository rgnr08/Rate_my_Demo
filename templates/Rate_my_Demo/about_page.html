What's it all about?

  • Rate My Demo is a platform for unsigned artists to get noticed.
  • If you're a solo musician, in a band, or even just a casual listener, there's something here for everyone.
  • Experience our randomiser function - we give you 5 random tracks of unsigned music. Don't like them? Hit randomise again for more!



What's our approach?

  • We don't like spam, you don't like spam. We present a platform that allows you to listen, vote, and favourite.
  • If you're a musician, benefit from honest streamlined feedback with no bias.
  • We're passionate about promoting true unsigned talent. You won't find any big names or established artists here, just a place to showcase new, emerging talent.
